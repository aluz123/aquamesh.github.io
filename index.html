About Us

AquaMesh began as an AP Research project, evolving into a UCSD startup focused on sustainability, public health, and innovative water quality solutions. From grant-funded research to real-world impact, we’re redefining how communities monitor their water sources.

  • Mission: Provide accessible, accurate, and scalable water monitoring solutions.
  • Vision: Ensure clean water for every community.
  • Recognition: Awarded multiple grants, partner to leading environmental agencies.

Our Products

Technology

AquaMesh combines cutting-edge optical sensing with secure mesh networking to provide real-time, high-fidelity data. Our patent-pending MagWipe™ cleaning technology ensures each sensor stays free from biofouling, reducing maintenance intervals.

  • Optical Sensing: Advanced photometric and spectrographic measurements.
  • Mesh Networking: Automatic node discovery and self-healing links.
  • Cloud Analytics: Scalable storage and AI insights.
