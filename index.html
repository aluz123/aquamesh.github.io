About Us

AquaMesh began as an AP Research project and evolved into a pioneering startup at UCSD. We focus on sustainability, public health, and innovative water quality solutions that empower communities and industries alike.

  • Mission: Provide accessible, accurate, and scalable water monitoring solutions.
  • Vision: Ensure clean water for every community.

Technology

We combine advanced optical sensing with secure mesh networking to deliver real-time, high-fidelity water quality data. Our innovative MagWipe™ cleaning technology ensures sensors remain clear, reducing maintenance and ensuring accuracy.

  • Optical Sensing: High-precision photometric and spectrographic measurements.
  • Mesh Networking: Self-healing, automated node discovery.
  • Cloud Analytics: Scalable storage and AI-driven insights.
