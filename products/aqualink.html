More Information

AquaLink™ is a smart mesh hub designed for rugged, autonomous deployments. Collect and transmit data from AquaSpectra sensors across remote regions.

  • Multi-hop LoRa mesh communication
  • Solar-powered with 30,000 mAh battery
  • Plug-and-play with AquaView dashboard
  • Mountable on buoys, poles, or tripods
  • Supports 1–12 AquaSpectra units
  • Uplink via LTE or WiFi
  • Connectivity: LTE, WiFi, RS-485
  • Battery: 30,000 mAh rechargeable
  • Power Input: Solar (20W)
  • Enclosure: IP67 field-tested casing
