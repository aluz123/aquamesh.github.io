The AquaSpectra™ sensor delivers lab-grade water chemistry insights from compact, field-ready hardware. Designed for environmental researchers and smart water infrastructure alike.

  • Dual-mode full-spectrum spectrometer + fluorometer
  • Swappable optical cartridges for different parameters
  • Built-in storage and LoRa wireless telemetry
  • Real-time nitrate, TOC, fDOM, chlorophyll, and turbidity monitoring
  • Watershed research, agricultural runoff, coastal sensing
  • Smart city infrastructure and IoT integration
  • Detection Range: 220–720 nm absorbance, 350–650 nm fluorescence
  • Storage: 32 GB SD card, USB-C export
  • Sampling: 1–60 min intervals or triggered
  • Enclosure: Waterproof IP68, corrosion-resistant
