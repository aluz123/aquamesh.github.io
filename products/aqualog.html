Aqualog™ Handheld Viewer

A portable, all‑in‑one spectrometer viewer with live graphs, onboard display, and USB‑C connectivity.

Key Features

Onboard Touchscreen

3.5″ color display with real‑time graphs and numeric readouts.

Rechargeable Battery

8‑hour runtime with fast USB‑C charging.

Easy Controls

Four tactile buttons for menu navigation and data capture.

Wireless Sync

Bluetooth LE for data upload and remote configuration.

Specifications

  • Display: 3.5″ IPS touchscreen, 480×320 px
  • Connectivity: USB‑C (data & charging), Bluetooth LE
  • Battery: 3,500 mAh rechargeable Li‑Ion (8 h runtime)
  • Sensor Modes: Real‑time spectral and fluorescence graphs
  • Storage: Internal 8 GB flash; export via USB or wireless
  • Dimensions: 160 × 70 × 30 mm
  • Weight: 250 g
  • Operating Temp: 0 °C to 50 °C
  • Enclosure: IP67‑rated polymer housing
